Python Google Stock Price Analysis and Forecast

  • Project Github Page

The data journey...

Using historic Google stock price data from the last five years, I explored five areas:
Visualize stock price trends over time
Calculate daily returns and assess volatility
Apply moving averages (e.g., 50-day, 200-day) to identify trends
Analyze trading volume patterns
Practice time series forecasting and financial modeling

After loading Pandas and Matplotlib and the data set, I inspected in to understand what cleaning was needed.
I convert the 'date' column to datetime type and installed it as the index. I then removed any NA data to prevent errors

As can be seen from the image above, I plotted the price changes over the five year period. This can be viewed along side
'.describe' function to understand the high and low prices and volumes across the data set.

I then plotted volatility by creating a new column with populated with the daily price change. This can be seen above.

Below, two moving averages can be seen, 50 and 200 days. I created these using the '.rolling' function. I plotted these
alongside the price to bette undestand the fluctuations in price.

Below, I plotted a chart showing the volume of shares traded each day, alongside the 7 and 14 day volume moving averages.
This chart shows the last 9 months for clarity.

Finally, I imported ARIMA to run statstical modelling and forecast future stock prices. I forecasted the next 10 days prices
using the previus 21 days data.
